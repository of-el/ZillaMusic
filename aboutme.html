About myself

I am a 24 year old music producer turned software engineer. I was born and raised in the Central Valley, more specifically Livingston, Ca. I was a normal kid growing up. Did well in school and tried staying busy with after school activities such as sports and in high school I joined the marching band and drumline. I attended UC Davis for a year, but left to try and figure out what it is I really wanted to do with my future. I spent a year at community college to get lower division courses out of the way, and in that time I really dove into producing music. I fell in love with it. I was once again able to work with music. In high school I had started a band of my own, but we split up going our separate ways. Music production on my laptop allowed me to stay in touch with my passion. After taking a couple music production courses at Cogswell, I realized that music was just my hobby, and I didn't want to mix business with pleasure. At the same time, my curiosity for understanding how things worked, led me to software development. I wanted to learn how the tools I used for music production on my laptop were created. At first it was all a mystery, but my time at Cogswell studying software has enriched my knowledge.
